Spend around 2-hour drive to My Tho. Explore the beautiful countryside by sampan & rowboat on this discovery expedition into the lush Mekong Delta. Your crew is set to welcome you aboard and sets off on a leisurely cruise on the Mekong River. From the boat’s unique vantage point, observe life as it is lived along the river – a fascinating procession of boats both big and small, casting their nets, and brilliant landscapes in every shade of green.

Stop along the way to visit local family businesses manufacturing honey tea, banana wine, and the traditional way to harvest honey and pick fruit.

Board a small rowing sampan to explore the diversity of the ecosystem along the riverbanks and the narrow and tranquil armlets lined with nipa palm trees. End your day by stopping at the local family business making coconut candy.

Drive you back to Ho Chi Minh City.
